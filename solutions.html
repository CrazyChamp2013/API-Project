💡 How We Can Be Superheroes!

Good news! While we can't stop criminals all at once, we can do a lot of things to make our cars safer and keep Fremont awesome!

🛠️ Things You Can Do Right Now:

  • 🔒 Lock It Up! Always lock every door! Even if you think it's locked, check it twice. Thieves are smart and sneaky!
  • 🔔 Use Your Alarm: Make sure your car alarm is set up and working. A loud alarm is like a big "NO!" sign to thieves.
  • 💨 Clear the Stuff: Don't leave anything valuable visible—not even loose change, charger cables, or backpacks. If they can't find stuff, they are less likely to steal!
  • 👀 Look Around: When you park, look at your surroundings. Do you see any sketchy people hanging around? Report it if you see something suspicious!
  • 📞 Call for Help: If a theft happens, call the police right away! Quick reporting helps catch criminals fast.

Let's keep Fremont safe and wonderful for everyone! Be smart, be safe!
