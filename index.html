Problem: Car Break-Ins Are Increasing in Our Area!

Our community has seen more car break-ins recently. This website shares information, prevention tips, and ways we can work together to stay safe.

What’s Happening?

Many cars in our neighborhood are being broken into, especially at night. Thieves are targeting parked cars and stealing valuables like laptops, bags, and electronics.
This creates fear and loss for families in our community.

🔗 🚗 Learn More

Why It Matters?

Car break-ins don’t just cause financial loss — they also make people feel unsafe.
When crime increases, it affects the entire community, including kids and families.

🔗 🚗 Learn More

Solutions / Prevention Tips

  • Always lock your car
  • Do not leave valuables inside
  • Park in well-lit areas
  • Install security cameras
  • Report suspicious activity
