🔍 Why Did Things Change?

The numbers show that 2023 was the biggest challenge year!

  • 🚨 **The Main Reason:** A huge part of the spike in 2023 was because of a national trend involving cars like Hyundai and Kia that weren't properly protected (some people called it the "Kia Challenge"). Fremont saw over 1,200% more thefts compared to the year before!
  • ⬇️ **Great News:** The data shows things are improving! By 2025, thefts had dropped down from their 2023 peak, which shows that all the hard work is helping!

🛡️ How Fremont PD is Fighting Back

The Fremont Police Department (FPD) and local partners are working hard to keep our community safe! Here is what they have been doing:

  • 🔑 **License Plate Readers:** They use special cameras to track any suspicious vehicles entering or leaving the city.
  • 🚔 **Targeted Patrols:** Police are increasing patrols in high-crime spots and running secret investigations to catch thieves!
  • ⚖️ **Smart Partnerships:** FPD is working closely with the District Attorney's office to make sure people who steal repeatedly get caught and charged properly.
  • 💰 **Stopping Big Crime:** Investigators have taken down major organized crime rings that deal in stolen electronics, helping reduce smaller burglaries.
  • 📢 **Education Campaigns:** They run public campaigns reminding everyone to never leave valuables visible and encourage businesses to install security cameras.
  • 🛡️ **Car Safety Help:** FPD encourages Hyundai/Kia owners to use safety upgrades, get software updates, and add steering wheel locks.

💚 Is Fremont Safer? And What Should I Do?

The short answer is: Yes! The trend is getting better after the big spike in 2023. But we can't stop thefts, so prevention is still super important!

💡 Safety Checklist for Parking in Fremont:

  • 📍 **Where are the dangers?** Pay extra attention to apartment lots, shopping centers, hotels, and park-and-ride areas.
  • 🗑️ **The Golden Rule:** NEVER leave backpacks, laptops, purses, or even loose change visible inside the car. Thieves look for easy targets!
