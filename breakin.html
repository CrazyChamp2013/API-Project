Hey, Fremont friends and neighbors! We all love our community, but we also need to talk about something serious: car theft. It's a huge problem that affects everyone!

😱 What is Happening in our Community?

Imagine you spend time and money on your car, and then someone takes it! That feels awful, right? This is what is happening in Fremont neighborhoods.

  • **Windows Smashed:** When you leave your car parked, thiefs smash windows, steal license plates, tool boxes, tires, expensive parts.
  • **Targets unlocked cars or visible items:** Valuables left in plain sight, Bags, laptops, phones or even coins attract attention!
  • **Happens mostly at night:** Stepping out at night is dangerous when such thieves are on the street looking for cars.
  • **Increasing in frequency:** Smash and Grab incidents are increasing day by day. Often happens in seconds, even in daylight!

Let's keep Fremont safe and wonderful for everyone! Be smart, be safe!
